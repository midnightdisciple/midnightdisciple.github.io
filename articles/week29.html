If I Could End The Universe

If I could end the universe
I think I’d do it slowly
Break it all down piece by piece
So I could know it fully

I’d tear apart the cosmos
Sear away the stars
Push light through each black hole
Heal all the cosmic scars

I’d gather up the planets
And take them out of play
Grind comets into space dust
Then blow them all away

I’d unravel all the elements
Untether time and space
Unthread DNA itself
Unmake the human race

I’d free up all the energy
Then turn off the light
Finally step outside the emptiness,
Close the door, and lock it tight
