Speaker 2
What seems to be
And bothers me
The thing I think I know
The truth that lies inside what’s shown
A knowing I can’t know
Nor own
The unknown known holds knowings
From it these knowings grow
They grow just as the first did
To grow and see and know
Yet more below
It’s such a deeply backwards knowing
To invoke
That perhaps you’ll think me broke
For merely saying reeks of playing
Though I spoke the way I know
Not play nor show
Its pains me dearly
To name clearly
Or in any way evoke
This knowing that I know
And somehow don’t
In the knowing’s game
From empty known to known
The same
A trick is played
Just unknown knowing blowing smoke
Perhaps its empty nothingness
Is only a sick joke
