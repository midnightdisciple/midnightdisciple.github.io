Complaint

Regarding what we see as the heinousness and severity of actions carried out by the defendant, the being referred to as Hunter Mangus, we beseech this court for remedial action. Such action is justified in light of the defendant’s alleged violation and subsequent murder of one Jane Doe (victim’s name withheld for privacy).

The defendant’s actions and their dark nature are known to this court via the contents of the so-called “Memory Hole” which defendant repeatedly uses for purposes of obfuscating these events as well as their true causes. The prosecution holds that this hole as well as the corresponding obfuscation’s true purpose is to avoid incrimination and therefore constitutes strong evidence of the defendant’s guilt.

Seeking a necessary and just resolution, we propose the court grant such relief in the form of the immediate termination of the defendant’s life.
