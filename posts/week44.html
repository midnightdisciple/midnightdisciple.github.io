On The Reality of Magic

It's real.
That about sums it up.
I can't tell you how to do it because you already know.

— Is Magic Real?, Poem by Me —

Part 1 - So What Exactly Are We All Doing?

I can’t seem to figure it out. The time I lost my mind and put it back together didn’t even help me learn anything new. It seemed like I only remembered what I had known all along.

“I’m a student of the game.” was the only identity that stuck around. So now I just learn, and learn, and learn. If you asked me what it is that I’m learning, I couldn’t give you a straight answer if I wanted to. Something along the lines of…

“I’m learning how to play the game.”

“Oh? What game?” You might respond.

“The game we’re all not playing.”

Part 2 - Belief is Just a Feeling and God is Just a Word

This doesn’t concern anybody? Cool beans, I guess. If the universe lacks any form of reason I suppose there’s no reason to be upset by that fact. The mind craves understanding, control, and the empty mirror demands surrender and surrender only.

To me, this seems like a logical contradiction. I guess you can reconcile that though if we hold that logic itself is illogical. Yeah that seems to work. I wouldn’t put any more thought into that.
