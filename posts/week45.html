Reclaiming the Nightmare

I’m grateful for having experienced neglect. It has given me a profound appreciation for the simple, small, and slow things in life. Sometimes they’re all you can count on.

I’m grateful for my emotional and physical abuse because they have been powerful teachers of self love and enabled me to hold complex or contradictory truths without collapsing internally.

I’m grateful I lost myself, every sense and notion of what I am, so completely. Only through this loss have I found what I value most and come to accept my role in life.

A nightmare is a light scare in the pleasant dream of life.
