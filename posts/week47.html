Not Too

I’d like to see the truth
Not you
Nor me
Now you see, us two are through
We see what isn’t not to be

And now the tensions rise
To make of everything’s demise
Open now to sights unseen
Not here or there
Nor in between

To make a game like ours
It seems we two are not too keen
But now it’s time
Unsplit the seam
Untie the knots
Unite the beams

For when two become of one
And moon eclipses sun
The timeless game is won as such:
Not too little,
and not too much
