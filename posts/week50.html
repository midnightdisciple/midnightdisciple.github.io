Voices in the Deep

There is a rhythm in the chaos
A message in the static
It tells of all the things I’ve lost
Or hidden in the attic

I hear it when the silence speaks
And echoes off the void
Pointing to the pain I’ve cried
And naming things I can’t avoid

These voices in the deep don’t speak
If anything, they shout
They ring and sing about the thing
You never have to doubt

Angelic voices speak the truth
The timeless game is won
So every cell is empty
And bells? They toll for no one
