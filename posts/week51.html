Bonus, a Conversation between The Christ and The Buddha

“Enlightenment,” Jesus began, “always seemed kind of like a dark joke. Everything is perfect, and you’re not even around to see it.” He slowly cracked a smile as he spoke.

The Buddha thought for a moment then matched The Nazarene’s expression before speaking.

“Maybe so.” Buddha responded, “Though darker still is the Buddhist’s charge to embrace their suffering as opposed to the Christian’s charge to merely endure it.”

“Enduring suffering grows one’s faith, embracing suffering increases the depths of one’s presence.” Christ synthesized, “I suppose that makes one thing we can agree on.”

“The trials are the blessings.”
