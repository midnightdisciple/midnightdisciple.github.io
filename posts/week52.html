A Curse For Those Who Fly Into The Sun

Is a blessing for anyone else.
So I decided to write them both.

The Curse

You are going to die.

In miniature tonight, and every night before you do the real thing.
All of the things you dislike about yourself are completely uninteresting and totally fine.
Your life today, and any other day for that matter, could be hellish in makeup.
You don’t have to want to change it.

Your life is a game that a stupid person could play and still win.
So if you if you ever catch yourself thinking about getting smart, remember.

You are going to die.

The Blessing

To those of you for which the earth is not enough, I leave these final words.

Let your resplendent wings carry you to new heights and beyond.
Let the ever changing winds guide the path of your ultimate ascent.
Let the sun, the source of light itself, be your target.
Let your aim be singular and true.
Let your eternal light so shine.

And when you fall,
And you will fall,

Let the sea be there to catch you.
